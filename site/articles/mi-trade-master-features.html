MI Trade Master App Features — Full Overview

By MI Traders · App Guide

MI Trade Master (sometimes searched as "Trading Master" or "Trade Master") is a free Android app built for one purpose: helping people actually understand trading instead of guessing their way through it. Here's a full look at what's inside.

Structured Courses

Step-by-step curriculum

Instead of scattered YouTube videos and random tips, the app organizes trading education into a structured path — starting from the basics of price charts and working up to more advanced concepts like risk-reward ratios and trade psychology.

AI Chart Analyzer

Instant chart feedback

Upload or point the analyzer at a chart, and it highlights patterns, trend direction, and key levels — helping learners connect what they've studied in the courses to real chart examples.

Risk-Free Practice Mode

Practice without real money

New traders can place practice trades in a simulated environment that mirrors real market conditions, building habits around position sizing and stop-loss placement before any real capital is involved.

Urdu & English Support

Learn in your own language

The app is built with Urdu-speaking learners in mind alongside English, so language isn't a barrier to understanding trading concepts clearly.

Who This App Is For

MI Trade Master is aimed at people starting from zero — no prior trading background required. It's also useful for traders who've picked up a few habits from unreliable sources and want to rebuild their understanding on a more structured foundation.

Getting Started

The app is a free direct APK download for Android. Since it isn't distributed through the Play Store yet, Android may show a Play Protect warning during install — this is expected for direct APK installs and can be bypassed from the install screen.
